I am Matthew Regidor

Actively writing code and building high-functioning teams.

Hey there! I'm Matthew. I worked in manufacturing at Tesla for 7 years where I got to witness firsthand the many innovations being made in the automotive industry and sustainable energy. In my last year at Tesla, I was promoted to Engineering Technician and worked closely with the engineers at the Tesla HQ in Palo Alto. It was here that I had the privilege of playing a small role in the launches of Megapack and Powerwall 3.

This experience inspired me to pursue a career in software engineering where I could make a greater personal contribution to solving problems with creativity. I was hooked after my first Python course and wanted to further my education by enrolling in App Academy. I graduated from their 16-week full-stack software engineering boot-camp in January 2024 and am excited to see where this journey will take me!
